Bio

Ben Folds is an American singer-songwriter and musician from Greensboro, North Carolina. From 1993 to 2000, he played as the front man in his namesake piano pop band, Ben Folds Five. With his flamboyant irreverence, he established himself as the Elton John for American Gen-Xers. Ben briefly reunited with the band in 2010 and continues to tour.
